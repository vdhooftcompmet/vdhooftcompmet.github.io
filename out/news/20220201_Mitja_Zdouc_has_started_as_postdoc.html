Mitja Zdouc has started as postdoc
2022-02-01

Mitja Zdouc has stared as postdoctral-researcher with Justin van der Hooft and Marnix Medema on the MARBLES-EU project. He will be working with them to predict chemical features from biosynthetic gene clusters, in order to use there for matching with MS/MS data. Please give him a warm welcome!
